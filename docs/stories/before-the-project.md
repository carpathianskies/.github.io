## Writing Tips for Your Stories

- Each story should be **2,000-5,000 words** to maintain pacing
- Include **sensory details** to immerse readers in the world
- Plant **subtle foreshadowing** about The Project's arrival
- Explore **moral ambiguity** — characters shouldn't be purely good or evil
- End with **unanswered questions** that the player will encounter later
